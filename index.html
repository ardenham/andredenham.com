About

Shaping the Future of Higher Education

With a career spanning academic leadership, teaching, and research, I bring a comprehensive perspective to the challenges and opportunities facing higher education today. My administrative experience has given me the tools to lead with vision, build high-performing teams, and drive meaningful institutional change.

As a full professor, I remain deeply connected to the academic mission — mentoring the next generation of scholars and practitioners while contributing to my field through ongoing research and publication. I believe that the best administrators never lose sight of what happens in the classroom and the lab.

I am passionate about creating equitable, innovative, and student-centered environments where both faculty and students can thrive.
